Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Mannai HVAC WhatsApp Assistant ("the Service") handles information. The Service is an internal informational tool that responds to messages from authorized users with documents, links, and answers to questions.

1. Information We Handle

When you send a message to the Service through WhatsApp, we process the content of that message in order to understand your request and provide a relevant response. This may include your WhatsApp phone number and the text of your messages.

2. How We Use Information

Information is used solely to:

  • Match your message to the correct document, link, or pre-set response.
  • Generate a relevant answer to your question.
  • Operate and maintain the Service.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information. To operate, your message may be processed by trusted service providers (such as WhatsApp/Meta for message delivery and an AI provider for generating answers) strictly for the purpose of responding to you.

4. Data Retention

Messages are processed to generate a response and are not retained beyond what is necessary to operate the Service. We do not build long-term profiles of users.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information handled by the Service. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

6. Your Choices

You may stop using the Service at any time by no longer sending messages to it. If you wish to request information about data handling, contact us using the details below.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
